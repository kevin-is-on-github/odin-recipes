Scrambled Egg

Description

A perfectly cooked fried egg can upgrade practically anything, from avocado toast to a double cheeseburger and beyond.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp. butter, or fat of your choice
  • large egg
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Lightly beat the eggs
  2. 3/4 teaspoon salt and a few grinds of black pepper in a medium bowl.
  3. Melt 1 tablespoon of the butter in a medium nonstick skillet over low heat
  4. Swirl to coat the bottom and sides.
  5. Add the eggs, and cook slowly, scraping them up with a rubber spatula occasionally, until most of the liquid has thickened and the eggs are soft, about 10 minutes.
  6. (If you like your eggs a little firmer, cook them for an additional 2 to 3 minutes.)
  7. Remove them from the heat, and gently fold in the remaining 1 tablespoon of butter.
  8. Serve hot.
